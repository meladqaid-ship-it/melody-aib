# 🎵 Melody AI - AI-Powered Music Generation Platform

Transform your words into professional songs with cutting-edge AI technology.

## ✨ Features

- 🎤 **Text to Song**: Enter lyrics and generate complete songs
- 🎙️ **Voice Recording**: Record your voice directly in browser
- 📤 **Audio Upload**: Upload existing audio for transformation
- 🎸 **10+ Genres**: Pop, Rap, Rock, EDM, Arabic, Khaleeji, Yemeni, and more
- 🎭 **6 Moods**: Happy, Sad, Epic, Romantic, Emotional, Motivational
- 🌍 **Multi-language**: English & Arabic support
- 🎚️ **Custom Duration**: 30s to 180s songs
- 👨‍👩 **Voice Selection**: Male and female voices
- 💰 **Flexible Pricing**: Free to Enterprise plans
- 📊 **Dashboard**: Track usage, credits, and history
- 🔒 **Enterprise Security**: JWT, rate limiting, encryption
- 🚀 **Production Ready**: Docker, CI/CD, monitoring
